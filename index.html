Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is this ROI calculator?

The calculations use industry benchmarks from our analysis of 200+ small businesses. Your actual results depend on your specific conversion rates, client values, and call volume. The calculator provides a conservative estimate — most businesses find the real number is higher because it doesn't account for referrals from captured clients.

What counts as a "missed call"?

Any call that goes to voicemail, rings out, or reaches someone who can't help. This includes after-hours calls, calls during lunch breaks, calls when staff is with other patients/clients, and weekend calls. For most businesses, 30-50% of incoming calls go unanswered.

How does an AI chatbot capture missed calls?

An AI chatbot on your website answers visitor questions instantly, 24/7. It captures name, email, phone number, and reason for visit — then books appointments or sends you the lead. It handles the same questions your receptionist answers, but never takes a break, never calls in sick, and costs a fraction of the price.
